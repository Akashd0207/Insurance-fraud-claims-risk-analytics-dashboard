# Motor Insurance Fraud, Claims & Risk Analytics Dashboard

## Project Overview

Developed an end-to-end Power BI dashboard to analyze over 15,000 motor insurance claims, identify potential fraud patterns, and assess risk indicators across policyholders, vehicles, and claim characteristics. The dashboard enables fraud investigators and insurance analysts to monitor fraud trends, investigate suspicious claims, and identify high-risk customer segments through interactive visualizations and drill-down analysis.

## Business Insights

* Policyholders with previous claim history exhibited higher fraud rates.
* Older vehicles showed increased fraud concentration compared to newer vehicles.
* Certain policy types demonstrated elevated fraud risk.
* Claims filed shortly after policy inception indicated higher fraud propensity.
* Fraud patterns varied across demographic, vehicle, and policy segments.
* Specific combinations of vehicle age, policy type, and prior claim history contributed disproportionately to fraudulent claims.

## Project Outcomes

* Identified key fraud indicators using interactive analytics and drill-down capabilities.
* Enabled efficient investigation of suspicious claims through risk-based segmentation.
* Improved visibility into fraud trends, claim behavior, and policyholder risk characteristics.
